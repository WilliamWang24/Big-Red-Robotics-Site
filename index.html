ABOUT

The Big Red Robotics Team (#11166) is the FTC Robotics' team for The Lawrenceville School. Going into our 7th year during the 2023-2024 season we hope to promote STEM and robotics in our local community in New Jersey and our student body as a whole through FTC. We aim to also develop a competitive robot for the season.

OUTREACH

No Child Left Behind - Lawrence Community Center (4/12)

Worked with 15 students aged 10-12. Brought ROBOTIS kits level 1 and built the type 11 robot. We also arranged driving activities with the 2022-2023 competition robot and the turret chasis. The event lasted from 1.5 hours.

JV Robotics Program - Lawrenceville School (Winter-Spring)

Opened a beginner-focused robotics program for students to understand the basics of FTC through hands-on experience with GoBilda and Vex Robotics parts.
